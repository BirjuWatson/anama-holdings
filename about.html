About Anama Holdings

Established in 2018 and based in Pretoria, Anama Holdings (Pty) Ltd has steadily expanded across the African continent by delivering turnkey solutions and developing local capacity through skills transfer and strategic partnerships.

Our Vision

To become the customer’s preferred choice, valuing quality, people and the environment.

Our Mission

To be leaders in service delivery and quality in construction, mining, infrastructure, energy and agriculture across Africa.

Core Values

  • Market-driven approach and international cost competitiveness
  • Investment in modern equipment and quality partnerships
  • Environmental compliance and safety-first operations
  • Integrity, accountability and community upliftment

Safety & Standards

We operate to international safety and quality management standards, applying ISO-like internal processes even where formal certification is in progress.

Strategy & Local Impact

Anama prioritises local hiring where possible, and implements tailored training programs to upskill workforces in project areas — combining practical delivery with social upliftment.
